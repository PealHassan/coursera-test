Bangladesh Cricket


       Recently Bangladesh is going through a critical corona pandemic. During their pandemic situation Bangladesh Cricket Board arranged a big tour in zimbabwe. One test, three odi's, three t-20's would be held during the tour.Bangladesh Cricket Board is taking this tour seriously. Because Bangladesh has recently passed a bad time with cricket against Newzealand and Srilanka. They lost three odi's against Newzealand and one test against Srilanka. So they are concerned about this tour. Although against Zimbabwe, Bangladesh team is favourite, they are taking this tour seriously.

Some recent facts about Bangladesh ODI Cricket:

lack of middle order batsman:

Bangladesh is facing this problem for the couple of time. The position 5 is given to Mohammed Mithun for a long time but he is unable to use this opputunity till now.

Bangladesh is facing a big trouble at their opening partnership for a long time. Liton das has shown a light in the beginning of his career. But recently he is facing problems to make runs and which make trouble for Bangladesh.Tamim Iqbal needs a good opening partnership to make a big total against opponents and gain a big chase.

Impressive Bowling Figure:

Bangladesh has improved their bowling condition a lot. They have improved their pace attack which is their biggest achieve. They have Mustafizur Rahman who is famous for his cutter and slow balls. Taskin Ahmed who is also a triumph card for team Bangladesh. He cad do good inswing, outswing, reverse swing, bounce and yorkers ball.Recently a new player named Saifuddin added who is also a good bowler. Shariful Islam, under 19 player, is also showing a light for team Bangladesh. In a whole, Bangladesh have created a good figure in their pace attack.

Their spin attack is so powerful for a long time. They have no.1 allrounder Shakib al Hassan and no.2 bowler Mehedi Hassan Miraz. Both of them have a impressive profile. So, Bangladesh is very good in their bowling attack.

lack of good fielding:

Bangladesh have a great problem in their fielding site. They are seemed to miss flat catches. They also seemed dizzy and idle at the time of fielding. Using this weakness, their opponents make 1 to 2 and 2 to 3 runs which cost a lot for the team at the last moment.
